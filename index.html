Greater Moving Co Site

Pack

Retrieve boxes, tissue paper, and packing material from warehouse. Pack all items safely and securely in boxes and label room and contents.

Move

Team divides into dolly workers and furniture wrappers. Stack boxes first at front of truck securely and add large, shrink wrapped and padded furniture items in back of truck.

Organize

Team divides into seperate rooms. Coordinate with owner as to where to unpack and organize contents of boxes.
